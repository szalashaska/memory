Welcome to the Memory

Memory is a simple card game. Your task is to find the matching pairs and to have fun while doing it! So let's have fun!

To spice things up we are keeping score, while you playing. The score takes into account how quick you are, so try to be as fast as possible. And if you find pairs in consecutive order, that also will be rewarded. The best players will be shown in our Top Scores table. But only if they register first, so there is no stranger on the board.

To make game even more interesting you get to choose your own theme of cards. Just type anything you like and we will try to find. If you do not feel like typing anything just use the "Quick Start" button.

Did you like the photos behind cards? Check them out after the game! For those, who decide to register, we can save the liked photos in our account.
